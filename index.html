About Us

Alabama Hiking Trails is a group of avid hikers who share a passion for exploring the great outdoors. We organize hikes and events throughout Alabama, and welcome hikers of all skill levels to join us on our adventures. Whether you're a seasoned hiker or just starting out, there's a place for you in our community.
